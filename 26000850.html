Safe practices
Things you can do to stay safe on social media.

Social media is fun, but it's important to stay safe while using it.

Top Safety Tips

No. Tip
1Use strong and unique passwords for each account.
2Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security.
3Keep your personal information private and think before posting.
4Don’t accept friend requests or DMs from strangers.
5Be cautious of phishing scams or suspicious links.
6Review and adjust your privacy settings often.
7Log out of accounts when using shared devices.

Quick Checklist

  1. Think before posting
  2. Check your privacy settings
  3. Use strong passwords

Key Points Summary

  • Protect personal info
  • Don’t click unknown links
  • Adjust settings regularly
